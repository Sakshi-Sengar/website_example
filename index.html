ABOUT

3D DRIVING CLASS

This is a simulation app that you can practice through games. You can practice the intestinal functional test and road test. The changed contents of the deduction points and the deduction items were actually applied to the game.
Get ready for racing at high speeds, hearing the sounds of burning engines, and top-notch drifting!

FEATURES

The App has superb racing controls and there are plenty of features to keep eyes glued to the screen for hours providing the player with a challenging and enjoyable experience.

Open World

Having an open-world element in a racing game. A walk-around feature adding more fun and depth and a fascinating ‘live the life’ component to motorsport.

Junior Categories

You work your way up through the ranks. You create your own motorsport path and move through the championships, just like in real life.

Flashback

Having the chance to rewind in a replay and go back to time before your car was destroyed by barrier. It save a lot of time and make games more accessible for new players

Random mini challenges

Provided with weird and wonderful in-game challenges.These kind of non-racing features can be fun and mix things up, making things less boring.

Damage

Not only more realistic car damage, but also on the maps and tracks(hitting a street light pole, bush or tyre stack will move, destroy or bring down those things).

More interaction

More interaction with the real-world elements like houses, people, buildings and businesses( that means a hit with the wall will cause some kind of issue with the car).
